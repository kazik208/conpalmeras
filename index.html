For rent: a holiday appartment in Torrevieja

Torrevieja is a town situated about 40 km from Alicante. It is a place specially favoured by tourists.
Over 300 sunny days yearly, Blue Flag beaches and wholesome microclimate thanks to two salty lakes Las Salinas de Torrevieja.

How to get there by car, by plane and how to move around.
Alicante can be reached by plane from over 70 cities in Europe (and beyond).
From Alicante the center of Torrevieja can be reached: by bus straight from the airport (then a local bus right to the house), by taxi or by a rented car.
In Torrevieja you can conveniently get around by local buses. The bus network is rather extended. A bike, an electric one too, can be rented. more

The best time to travel to Torrevieja is from May to October, when it is warm and hot, and the rains are scarce.
During winter the weather is also nice – days are quite long and sunny, but a break in the weather may occur – strong wind and rain.
Average temperature is highest in August 29°C,
lowest in February 15°C.
Water temperature is between 15°C and 27°C.
