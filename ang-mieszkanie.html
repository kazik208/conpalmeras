Quiet two bedroom appartment at Calle Francisco Pizarro corner of Calle Churrucca,
for 4 adults (with a possibility of one additional crib for a child 0-3).
Entrance to the building via a door with entryphone. From ground floor an elevator to the gallery with the entrance to the appartment. No stairs at all!
Appartment: 60 m2 on the 4th floor (entryphone, elevator), 3 rooms, bathroom, kitchen and closet. Simple and comfortable furniture.
Living/dining room 22 m2 with 2 couches, table, TV.
Master bedroom 11 m2 with a double bed 160x200 cm (with an option to seperate into two beds 80x200 cm).
Smaller bedroom 8 m2 with a working place and 2 beds (90x195 cm and 80x200 cm).
Kitchen 5 m2, no door to living room (ceramic plate, microwave oven, refrigerator, toaster, coffee maker).
Bathroom: shower/wash basin/bidet/WC. Warm water – boiler 50 liters.
Balcony 6 m2 (electric grill available). Balcony overlooking the sea.
In the appartment: high-speed Internet and Wi-Fi, washing machine, iron, hair dryer, garden furniture, shopping trolley, 2 beach chairs, a crib. Spanish TV.
A big swimming pool next to the house, available from June to September. 200 m to the sea.
Free parking on public parking places along Calle Pizarro and Calle Paseo Central.
Non-smokers only.
The guests are kindly requested to follow the rules.
